Programs & Classes

From swim lessons to senior fitness, Camp to martial arts, our programs bring the Sewickley Valley together at every age and stage. Explore what's happening at the Y and find your next favorite thing.

Fitness and Exercise

From group exercise and personal training to water fitness and our Wellness Center, we'll help you build strength, stay active, and feel your best, at any age or ability level.

Aquatics

Our indoor pools are home to swim lessons for every age, water exercise classes, swim teams, and lifeguard certification. Whether your family is just learning to swim or training to compete, our certified instructors are here to help.

Group Swim Lessons Adaptive Swim Lessons Private Swim Lessons Water Exercise Pool Reservations Swim Teams Lifeguard Certification

Sports, Rec, and Leagues

From toddlers to adults, our sports and recreation programs build skill, confidence, and community. Choose from youth and teen leagues, adult sports, martial arts, and recreational play for every age.

Preschool and Toddler Youth and Teen Sports Youth and Teen Recreation Adult Martial Arts

Child Care and Camp

Trust your child's care and growth to the Y. Kid Zone, before and after school care, early education, Sewickley Montessori School, and day camps give families across the Valley safe, nurturing options year-round.

Being part of the Y means being part of something bigger. Special events, Faith in Action, Senior Central, youth leadership programs, and food access initiatives like Quaker Valley Food to Families and our Free Mini Pantries reflect what we believe: for a better us, everyone belongs.
